# Participation Tracker (Partisipasi Komunitas)

Tracks and gamifies cooperative member attendance, voting engagement, and economic transaction participation.

---

### 📈 Metrics
- **Meeting Attendance**: Tracks member attendance rates in Assemblies (RAT) and training programs.
- **Decision Voting Rate**: Democratic engagement in policy voting and officer elections.
- **Economic Engagement**: Ratio of active business unit purchases/sales carried out by members.
- **Streaks**: Gamified active streaks of members carrying out consecutive activities.

---

### 🗓️ Community Activity Heatmap
Displays daily activities (assembly sessions, quizes, events) over a grid representing calendar days.
- Color codes represent low (muted border), medium (emerald-500/30), and high (emerald-500) attendance/interaction days.
