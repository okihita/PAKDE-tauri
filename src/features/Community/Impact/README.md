# Social Impact Screen (Dampak Sosial)

This screen measures the socio-economic and environmental contribution of the village cooperative, focusing on sustainability and member welfare.

---

### 📈 Social Impact Indicators
- **Local Employment**: Direct jobs created across all operational units (e.g. apothecary assistants, fertilizer staff).
- **Farmer Income Uplift**: Average crop yield and profit increase from seed distributions.
- **Eco Sustainability**: Tonnes of organic fertilizers supplied to replace chemical alternatives.
- **Gender Balance**: Woman ratio within cooperative officer boards and workforces.

---

### 📊 SROI Audit Model
- **Social Return on Investment (SROI)**: Quantifies the social value generated for every Rp 1 invested.
- Audits run live via a simulated interval scan analyzing membership, active business transactions, and event logs.
